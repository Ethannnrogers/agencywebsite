Local SEO For Small Business

What is Local SEO?

If you have a small business, (like a restaurant or a landscaping business) where people have to visit your physical location or you provide a service in a certain area, Local SEO is how the online presence of your business can be discovered by potential customers in your area. Local SEO is the process of optimizing the online presence of your business to better rank against competition in a specific area. Having an online presence gives a chance for the entire world to see your business, but without having it properly optimized no one will be able to find it. In summary: you need to have your online business information optimized so that consumers in your area can find you when they need your services.



Why is Local SEO Important?

There are literally hundreds if not thousands of people in your area searching for the services you provide. If your business information is displayed and properly optimized online, you open up the opportunity for all of those people to contact you and turn them into customers. If the online presence of your business isn’t optimized, Google is suggesting all of the searching consumers use your competitors.



How is Local SEO Accomplished?

There is a variety of ways that local seo is accomplished. A big part is to have a verified Google My Business listing. This ensures that your business will show up to people searching for services on location apps like Google Maps. All of your business information listed online needs to be linked together creating a sort of ‘web’ for your business making it easier for searching consumers to find you but more importantly helps your business rank.

The biggest and most challenging process is consistently fighting for ranking by generating positive customer reviews on Google, and fighting for keywords against competitors. This is a tedious process that requires a lot of dedicated time in knowing how the competitors rank compared to your ranking. Ranking high is one thing, making sure you stay ranking high is a completely different thing.
