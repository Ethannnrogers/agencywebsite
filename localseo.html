What is Local SEO?

Local SEO (Search Enginge Optimization) is the strategy and process of increasing your business ranking and online presence across Search Engines. Specifically ones like Google and Google Maps where users are searching for services that your business provides.



Ranking Top 3

Google only suggests the top 3 businesses when a consumer is searching for services. If your business fails to appear in Google Map's top 3 results, it is invisible to over 70% of web surfers.

Most imporantantly, good reviews help your business rank at the top against all of your competitors.
